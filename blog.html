The power of Lavender

This legendary herb belongs to the mint family, but its taste and aroma are absolutely unique. The strong, crisp scent is described as a mix of mint and rosemary with a rich, floral twist. The long history of lavender as a beauty and medical treatment suggests that lavender oil is far more than just a pretty plant.
Lavandula angustifolia has long been enjoyed in its oil form for its lavish scent and healing effects. Queen Elizabeth loved using lavender as a perfume, and even added it to her tea. Victorian ladies loved using lavender oil as perfumes, and it was even used as an antiseptic in the First World War. Today we celebrate lavender oil as a means to fight reduce stress, headaches and even keep good mood! We adore the lavender oil benefits for skin and take any chance we can get to slip it into our beauty routines.
